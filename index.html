Enterprise & Healthcare

Selling via corporate wellness programs, insurance companies, and healthcare providers to reach employees & patients.

Key Decision Makers

  • Healthcare Providers: Endocrinologists, nutritionists, diabetes educators

  • Corporate Benefits: HR directors, wellness coordinators

ROI & Benefits

  • Program Engagement: Interactive technology and real-time feedback drive significantly higher participation in wellness programs
